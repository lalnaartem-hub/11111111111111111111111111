Browser OS - zapusk
===================

1) Dvazhdy shchelknite:  start-dev.cmd
   Otkroite: http://localhost:5173/

2) Esli Node ne naiden - ustanovite s https://nodejs.org/

3) Oboi Kali: setup-wallpapers.cmd (rabotaet BEZ interneta - sozdaet SVG)

4) Temnaya tema:
   Nastrojki -> Oformlenie -> Temnaya
   Esli ne vidno raznicy - v Nastrojki -> Sistema vyberite macOS (ne Kali)

5) Ne zapuskajte "node ..." v PowerShell esli node ne v PATH -
   tolko start-dev.cmd i setup-wallpapers.cmd
